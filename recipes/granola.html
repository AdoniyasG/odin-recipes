Coconut Granola

This crunchy coconut granola is an easy recipe for a make-ahead, on-the-go breakfast, or midday snack.

Recipe by Maren Ellingboe King
Prep Time: Cook Time: Total Time:
10 mins 1 hrs 1 hrs 10 mins
Servings: Yield:
16 8

Ingredients

  • 5 cups rolled oats
  • 2 cups almonds
  • 2 cups unsweetened shredded coconut
  • 1 ¼ cups maple syrup
  • ½ cup coconut oil
  • 1 egg white
  • 2 tablespoons chia seeds
  • 1 teaspoon cinnamon
  • 1 teaspoon cardamom
  • 1 teaspoon kosher salt

Recipe instruction

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Line two rimmed baking sheets with parchment paper.
  2. Combine all ingredients in a large bowl. Use your hands or a wooden spoon to
    thoroughly mix, until oats and nuts are well coated. Spread mixture
    onto prepared baking sheets in an even layer.
    3. >
  3. Bake until golden brown, about 40 minutes. Stir granola and rotate pans 20 minutes
    into baking time so that it browns evenly.
  4. Cool completely, then transfer to lidded containers or jars. Store at room
    temperature for up to two weeks. Serve with milk, yogurt, or your choice of fruit
    (I like berries, peaches, mango or pineapple).
